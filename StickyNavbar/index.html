Coffee is one of the most popular beverages worldwide, enjoyed for its rich flavor and stimulating effects. Here’s an overview covering various aspects of coffee: History Origins: Coffee is believed to have originated in Ethiopia. Legend has it that a goat herder named Kaldi discovered the energizing effects of coffee beans. Spread: By the 15th century, coffee spread to the Arabian Peninsula, where it became a popular drink in Persia, Egypt, and the Ottoman Empire. Coffee houses, known as qahveh khaneh, became centers for social interaction. Europe and the Americas: Coffee reached Europe in the 17th century, where it quickly gained popularity. The first coffee house in England opened in Oxford in 1650. Coffee was introduced to the Americas in the 18th century.
